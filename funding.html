Financial Planning

Funding & Applications

Grant strategies, fellowship applications, and financial planning.

USAL tuition is remarkably affordable (~€300–550/year tutela académica after first year), making the financial barrier low. The main cost is living expenses.

Funding Strategy

Fully Funded European

Primary bid: La Caixa INPhINIT Incoming fellowship (~€26,400/yr + tuition + travel). Backup: MSCA Doctoral Networks, FPI contracts.

Tier-1

Artist-Scholar Hybrid

Combine Fulbright (if US-based application year), Creative Capital, USA Fellowship, Fromm Foundation, Barlow Endowment.

Creative

Lean Start

Self-fund Year 1 at ~€300-550 tuition + living costs, while applying for Year 2+ funding. USAL's low cost makes this viable.
